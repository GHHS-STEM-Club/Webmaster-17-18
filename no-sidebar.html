Education Applications

For Students, Teachers, and Administrators

Many schools across the country have very low test scores. This is no new issue as well- many schools across America consistently have low test scores in Math, Sciences and English. This can be explained by the teaching system today. For example, a teacher teaches a class every day. That teacher gives out the same information to each student. While this works for the most part, many students struggle because they’re not getting individualized work on concepts they specifically don’t understand.

Here at AIM, we are happy to announce the creation of M81: VIRGO

Using our brand new VIRGO software, M81 is able to help students succeed in a wide variety of subjects. M81 is able to analyze individual student’s performance in different areas of math and other subjects, and with communication and class-wide instruction from the teacher, is able to create an individual program for each student.

M81: VIRGO allows students to work with a tailor-made program to help them understand concepts they don’t fully understand; giving them higher test scores and more self-confidence.

M81: VIRGO allows the teacher to know what everyone is primarily struggling with, and helps inform the teacher what to teach before a test. Useful for most subjects- 6-8 Math, Algebra, Geometry/Trigonometry, Calculus, Statistics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, AP programs, and many more
