Synopsis

At AIM (Andromeda Intelligence Machines) we have only one objective, and that is to create the most powerful and utilitarian AI of all time. That is, we are going to fabricate the future. With each technological advancement and every inch of support from our backers, AIM will pave the way to a new age of innovation and invention.

As a company, our goals are to assist our clients in personal, educational, and commercial applications. Some examples of personal use would be helping file taxes, paying bills, optimizing shopping opportunities and much more. Why spend countless hours painfully filling out documents where you could be doing something much more enjoyable? In terms of education, our AI can be applied to the classrooms k-12 and throughout college, ranging from teaching the alphabet and counting, all the way to an Ivy League lecture hall. Furthermore, the AI appertains to a business setting, where it is able to analyze the company’s statistics to determine the best possible route for them to reach a successful destination, and providing a step by step plan to reach that goal. Lastly, each model of this AI is capable of any type of research, from a third grade animal report to a highly sensitive space operation.
